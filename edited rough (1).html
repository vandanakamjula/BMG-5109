Welcome to Ottawa E-Hospital appointment booking platform!

  1. Select the most convenient location for you
  2. Choose the test you would like to perform
  3. Give your contact details
  4. Book your appointment

You will receive a confirmation e-mail following your booking and SMS reminder along with e-mail 24 hours prior to your appointment date and time will be sent.

Please note that results will be available within 24 hours for any conventional tests and within 12 hours for general test.

*Suspicious results may take up to 36 hours
